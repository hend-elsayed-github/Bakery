About us

Baguette bakery, A Taste of Tradition Since 1990

Nestled in the heart of Alexandria, Egypt, Baguette bakery has been crafting artisanal breads and pastries with passion for over three decades. Whether you're craving the perfect golden-crusted baguette, a buttery croissant, or hearty loaves baked to perfection, we bring the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques to every bite. Looking for something special? We happily take custom orders to make your occasions extra memorable because every great gathering deserves the perfect bread. Visit us today and experience the warmth of fresh-baked goodness!
